# Monetisation Plan

## 1. Monetisation principle

Revenue should not damage the second-screen experience.

Nexus must remain fast, trustworthy, and premium. Ads and affiliates can work, but clutter will weaken retention and brand quality.

## 2. Phase 1: Early monetisation

### Display ads

Use carefully placed ads on guide/database pages, not intrusive map/AI surfaces.

Rules:

- No popups blocking gameplay help.
- No ads covering map controls.
- Avoid excessive above-the-fold ads.
- Monitor Core Web Vitals.

### Affiliates

Potential categories:

- Controllers
- Headsets
- Gaming monitors
- Consoles/accessories
- Capture cards
- Gaming chairs/desks
- Storage drives

Rules:

- Clearly disclose affiliate relationships.
- Keep recommendations relevant.
- Do not make fake reviews.

### Email sponsorships

Once newsletter has audience:

- Weekly GTA update
- Patch notes
- New discoveries
- Money route of the week

## 3. Phase 2: Premium

Potential premium features:

- Ad-free browsing
- Saved progress sync
- Advanced route planner
- Advanced money calculator
- Custom checklists
- Unlimited AI plans
- Private notes
- Early community features
- Cross-game account benefits

Pricing hypothesis:

- Monthly: low-cost gamer-friendly subscription
- Annual: discounted
- Lifetime founder tier possible early

Premium should enhance utility, not lock basic help behind paywalls.

## 4. Phase 3: Creator/community monetisation

- Creator pages
- Shareable maps/routes
- Creator guide embeds
- Sponsored creator challenges
- Contributor badges/reputation

## 5. Phase 4: API/data products

Possible later B2B/API:

- Game data API
- Map embed tools
- Creator widgets
- White-label companion tools
- Data licensing

Only after legal review and proprietary data advantage.

## 6. Revenue metrics

- RPM
- Affiliate CTR
- Affiliate conversion
- Newsletter sponsorship revenue
- Premium conversion
- Churn
- ARPU
- Revenue per active helped minute

## 7. Monetisation risks

- Ads reduce premium feel.
- Affiliates bias recommendations.
- Premium too early reduces growth.
- Sponsored content hurts trust.
- Trademark/copyright issues for data/API.

## 8. Revenue roadmap

### MVP

- Ad slots ready but minimal.
- Affiliate architecture ready.
- Email capture.

### Launch

- Activate ads on content pages.
- Add affiliate blocks to relevant gear pages only.
- Build newsletter.

### Retention

- Launch accounts/progress.
- Test premium ad-free + progress sync.

### Scale

- Sponsorships and API exploration.
